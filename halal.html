HALAL

CHIKIPOP Sdn. Bhd is concerned about the special nutritional needs of Muslims in Malaysia and is 100% committed to:

- Produce, distribute and supply food products consistently that comply with the latest halal requirements and regulations set by the Malaysian Islamic Development Department (JAKIM).

- Ensuring that all ingredients, production systems, kitchen equipment and utensils used in our restaurants in Malaysia are clean and free from non-halal elements so that customers can enjoy their meals without hesitation.

- Immediate action will be taken against any employee, supplier, and/or contractor found to be in breach of the agreed halal policy (intentionally or unintentionally).
