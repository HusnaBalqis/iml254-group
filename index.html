About Us

My family came up with the term ChikiPoP, which was derived from the word chicken, and Pop, which was derived from popcorn. The year of establishment of ChikiPoP was 2022. Possessing my own fast-food enterprise had long been a fantasy of mine. My family had emerged as my most ardent advocate in my pursuit to realise my ambition.
